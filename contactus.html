About Linda
Dogs have always been my passion, which makes dog grooming so much fun for me. At home I have a Boxer Mix, Chow Chow Mix and a Maltese Mix! I attended Clip Shoppe School of Dog Grooming where I became a certified licensed dog groomer. I am also Pet First Aid and CPR certified.
I attend grooming seminars at Grooming Expos and Trade Shows every chance I get to keep up with the latest grooming trends.
We offer exceptional services with an emphasis on the health, safety and comfort for your pet. Schedule your appointment today!
